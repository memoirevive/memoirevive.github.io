Mémoire Vive

FR
We explore forms of activism based on visualization, open-source and the collective.



Mémoire Vive was created in 2022, following our first action, @laviondebernard, launched on Instagram then Twitter. Thanks to public data emitted by planes via the ADS-B technology, these accounts track the flights and CO2 emissions of private jets of several personalities and companies.

We are currently developing the project Le pipeline de Patrick, cartographic monitoring of the construction works of the EACOP oil project of TotalEnergies in Uganda. The project is based on space imagery data from the European Space Agency's Sentinel project.
